Meet Zaco: Your Gateway to Tech Brilliance

Zaco Computers is a leading global provider of IT infrastructure solutions. For over a decade, we have helped thousands of organizations across industries optimize their IT environments.
We provide a wide range of new, pre-owned, and refurbished servers, storage, networking equipment, and spare parts from leading OEMs including HP, IBM, Oracle, Cisco, NetApp, EMC, and more. All inventory is fully tested and backed by a comprehensive warranty for peace of mind.
